Where Is Japan, Exactly?

Not sure where to start? Japan is an island nation in East Asia, nestled between the Pacific Ocean and the Sea of Japan. Many believe it's one of the most captivating places in the world, and they’re absolutely right!

Comprising four main islands—Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku—Japan boasts a stunning blend of ancient tradition and cutting-edge modernity. From the serene temples of Kyoto and the bustling streets of Tokyo to the breathtaking landscapes of Mount Fuji and the tropical beaches of Okinawa, Japan offers a rich tapestry of experiences waiting for you to explore.
