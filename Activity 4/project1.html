HUAWEI Matebook D14

The HUAWEI MateBook D14 (2021) is a sleek and lightweight laptop designed for productivity. With its 14-inch IPS display, powerful Intel Core i5 processor, and thoughtful features like a backlit keyboard and TÜV Rheinland certifications for eye comfort, it’s a great choice for everyday use. Whether you’re working, studying, or streaming, the MateBook D 14 delivers a seamless experience.

KEY FEATURES

11th Gen Intel® Core™ processor

The 11th Gen Intel® Core™ processor makes everything that much faster, so you spend less time waiting and more time doing. HD movies? Still a piece of cake! The Intel® Iris® Xe paints your favourite content in glowing colours, sending graphics performance through the roof.

Sleek Metal Body

Light, but Hardly Slight. At just 1.38 kg and 15.9 mm thick4, this sleek metallic laptop can take your on-the-go life to exciting new places. The hinged display opens all the way to 180°, folding to account for your every need and whim.

Super Device

Standout Productivity Drag-and-Drop for Multi-screen Collaboration Super Device facilitates seamless cross-device collaboration, allowing a vast array of smart devices to function as one cohesive unit. To form a Super Device, open the control center on your laptop, access the Super Device screen, and drag and drop to connect the devices.

Secure Pop-up Webcam

Activates only when you press it to pop up. Putting your privacy at your fingertips.
