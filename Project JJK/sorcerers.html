Sorcerers of Jujutsu Kaisen

Masamichi Yaga
The Principal of Tokyo Jujutsu High and a grade 1 jujutsu sorcerer.
Jujutsu: Cursed Corpses
Satoru Gojo
A special grade jujutsu sorcerer and recognized as the strongest in the world. The first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years
Jujutsu: Cursed Energy Manipulation
Shoko Ieiri
An alumna of Tokyo Jujutsu High, having been classmates with Satoru Gojo and Suguru Geto. After graduating into a full-fledged jujutsu sorcerer, Shoko became the school's primary doctor.
Cursed Technique: Reverse Cursed Technique
Kento Nanami
A former student of Tokyo Jujutsu High where he was an underclassman of Satoru Gojo and Suguru Geto.
Cursed Technique: Innate Technique
Yuji Itadori
The main protagonist of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is the son of Jin Itadori and Kaori Itadori, and the grandson of Wasuke Itadori.
Cursed Technique: Innate Technique
Megumi Fushiguro
A grade 2 jujutsu sorcerer and first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High alongside Yuji Itadori and Nobara Kugisaki.
Jujutsu: Cursed Energy Manipulation
Nobara Kugisaki
A first-year student and grade 3 jujutsu sorcerer at Tokyo Jujutsu High studying under Satoru Gojo alongside Yuji and Megumi.
Cursed Technique: Innate Technique
Maki Zenin
She is currently a second-year at Tokyo Jujutsu High. Daughter of Ogi Zenin and the elder twin sister of Mai Zenin, Maki was born in the Zenin Clan.
Equipment: Cursed Tools
Toge Inumaki
A descendant of the Inumaki clan and has inherited the clan's cursed speech, which he's developed his own unique speech pattern for in order to avoid cursing others.
Cursed Technique: Inherited Technique
Panda
Despite his name, Panda is not an actual panda, he's an Abrupt-Mutation Cursed Corpse created by Masamichi Yaga. He attends Jujutsu High as a second-year student along with Maki, Toge, and Yuta.
Jujutsu: Cursed Corpses Cores
Yuta Okkotsu
He was initially a special grade cursed human haunted by his late childhood friend, Rika Orimoto.
Cursed Technique: Rika
Utahime Iori
She is a semi-grade 1 jujutsu sorcerer and student supervisor at Kyoto Jujutsu High.
Cursed Technique: Innate Technique
Mai Zenin
A second-year at student at Kyoto Jujutsu High, a member of the Zenin clan and the younger twin sister of Maki Zenin.
Cursed Technique: Innate Technique
Kasumi Miwa
A second-year student at Kyoto Jujutsu High. A jujutsu sorcerer who uses the New Shadow Style swordsmanship techniques.
Cursed Technique: New Shadow Style Techniques
Aoi Todo
A third-year student at Kyoto Jujutsu High School and a grade 1 jujutsu sorcerer. He was mentored by Yuki Tsukumo in his youth and considers Yuji Itadori his best friend.
Cursed Technique: Innate Technique
Yuki Tsukumo
One of only four special grade jujutsu sorcerers. Yuki is not aligned with Jujutsu High.
Cursed Technique: Innate Technique
