About
What is Harmoodia?

Harmoodia is your personal mood-based music guide. Choose an emoji that reflects how you feel, and the app will instantly present a tailored song post complete with a direct Spotify link content, artist background, and a lyrics link. Everything is displayed in a smooth and minimalistic layout that feels familiar and engaging.

Pure Vibes, No Fuss

Whether you're feeling upbeat, low-key, or anything in between, Harmoodia helps you discover music that matches your emotional rhythm. No smartwatch or Spotify login required. Just pure vibes.

Inspired by My Smartwatch Project

This project was inspired by a previous activity that integrated a smartwatch with Spotify to detect moods and recommend songs. Harmoodia builds on that idea by creating a web-based experience that brings emotion-driven music discovery to everyone, without the need for wearables or logins.
